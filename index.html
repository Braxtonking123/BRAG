HITEK Ceramic window tinting
Ceramic tint is a premium window film that uses non-metal, non-conductive ceramic particles to block UV rays, heat, and light-glare. It offers superior performance in terms of heat rejection, reducing the interior temperature significantly while maintaining a clear view from the inside. Ceramic tints do not interfere with electronic signals such as GPS, mobile devices, or radios, making it ideal for modern vehicles. Ceramic tint also provides excellent UV protection, blocking up to 99% of harmful rays, which helps protect the passenger's as well as the car’s interior from sun-fading.
Ceramic Pro Available Shades 5%, 20%, 35%, 40%. 50%, 70%
HITEK Carbon window tinting
Carbon tinting is a durable, non-metallic option that uses carbon particles to create a matte finish. It offers effective heat and UV protection, keeping the interior cool while preventing sun damage. Unlike metallic films, carbon tint doesn’t interfere with electronic signals, and its deep color provides a sleek, non-reflective appearance. Carbon tints are known for their long-lasting quality, as they resist color fading over time. Although they don’t block as much heat as ceramic tints, they are a cost-effective alternative with a solid overall performance.
Carbon IR Available Shades 2%, 5%, 15%, 20%, 35%, 40%, 50%

When do I need a repair?

Any damage over 6 inches in length

The impact point is larger than a dime

The damage from the chip blocks any cameras or sensors.

Rock chips located directly within the driver's line of sight could result in visibility issues.

The chip has cracks radiating outwards, which indicates the damage is spreading and compromises the windshield's strength.

Your windshield has multiple chips, even if they're small, the cumulative damage can weaken the overall integrity of the glass.
