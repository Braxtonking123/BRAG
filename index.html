High Tech Ceramic Window Tinting
High tech ceramic tinting is a premium window film that uses non-metal, non-conductive ceramic particles to block UV rays, heat, and glare. It offers superior performance in terms of heat rejection, reducing the interior temperature significantly while maintaining a clear view from the inside. Ceramic tints do not interfere with electronic signals such as GPS, mobile devices, or radios, making them ideal for modern vehicles. They also provide excellent UV protection, blocking up to 99% of harmful rays, which helps protect both passengers and the car’s interior from fading
Carbon Tinting
Carbon tinting is a durable, non-metallic option that uses carbon particles to create a matte finish. It offers effective heat and UV protection, keeping the interior cool while preventing sun damage. Unlike metallic films, carbon tint doesn’t interfere with electronic signals, and its deep color provides a sleek, non-reflective look. Carbon tints are known for their long-lasting quality, as they resist fading over time. Although they don’t block as much heat as ceramic tints, they are a cost-effective alternative with solid overall performance
